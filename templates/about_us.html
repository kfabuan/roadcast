About Us

Roadcast is an incident management web application for the PNP police in Pasig City Police Station. The web application provides a number of data visualizations in graphs and charts, tabular reports, and forecasting of the city's road incident data. Roadcast makes road incident reporting, viewing, data storage, and retrieval more efficient. The web application allows for the transfer of data in a timely, secure, and trustworthy manner.

Why Roadcast?

To provide you instant access to information about the safety of your transportation network, for you to be more informed and vigilant on the road.

What's the goal?

The goal is to address issues by reporting it to the system, as well as to avoid future incidents by monitoring vehicular incidents and hotspots.

Who can use it?

The drivers, residents of Pasig city, citizens from other cities, and individuals who are just interested to know more about the happenings in Pasig.

Graphs and Charts

View data visualization diagrams of road incident reports to gain insights in crash patterns and trends.

Monthly Reports

Know the total number of road incidents in a month, statistics of people involved, vehicles, and more.

Real-time Forecasting

Determine the estimated number of road incidents that may happen in the city for the following days.

Incident Hotspot Map

Determine which areas in Pasig have the highest number of recorded road incidents.

View Incident Reports

Browse past and recently added road incident reports filed by people and the police in Pasig city.

Report to the Police
